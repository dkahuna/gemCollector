Mining for Gems
  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four gems below for you to choose from. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of "karats" to your total "K-ount".

  • You win the game by matching your total to the random karat amount. You lose if your total score goes past the karat amount given.

  • The value of each gem is random(hidden as well) from you until you click on it.

  • After succession or failure, a new game will present itself and continue tracking your wins&losses.

  • BEST OF LUCK!!
